Projects

National University of Singapore

Worked on developing a Deep Learning architecture for de-noising of Optical Coherence Tomography(OCT) images of the Optic Nerve Head (ONH).
The model was able to de-noise the image in about 80ms with good visual quality which is a 5x improvement from the available methods of de-noising.

CSIR-CEERI

Part of research group that worked on Statistical approach for classification of Plastic polymers. Partial Least Squares Discriminant Analysis (PLS-DA) was done on NIRS data comprising of various plastic polymers.
Results showed great promise with a high percentage of accurate classification >98% for all the classes of plastics considered.

Indian Institute of Technology, Madras

Developed a portal for dynamic car pooling services and researched means of using graph theory and clustering algorithms to group customer location data.
Implemented and studied the performance of Dynamic Programming and Greedy Algorithms for finding the shortest route.
